About Us

Palizi Capital is a boutique investment firm leveraging deep industry expertise and innovative strategies to drive business transformation, growth, and long-term value creation. With strong roots in Private Equity, Alternative Asset Management, and Capital Markets & Advisory, we serve institutional and individual investors through impactful, sustainable, and technology-driven solutions.

The word “Palizi” means weeding in Swahili - a powerful metaphor for how we operate: removing inefficiencies, eliminating underperformance, and clearing the path for businesses to thrive. We identify what holds companies back and enable accelerated, sustainable growth.

Our Mission

To unlock sustainable long-term value through strategic investment, innovation, operational excellence, and transformative partnerships that accelerate growth and impact.
