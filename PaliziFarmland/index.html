Palizi Farmland

Smart Farming. Shared Prosperity.

Palizi Farmland is a smart farmland asset-management platform that transforms underutilized and degraded land into profitable, climate-resilient farms.

What We Do

We provide a full-stack, Farm-as-a-Service (FaaS) platform to make farmland an equitable opportunity. Our model empowers smallholders, youth, and investors to participate in sustainable agriculture by delivering:

  • Fractional access to farmland.
  • End-to-end management of farm operations.
  • Smart regenerative farming practices.
  • Market integration for produce.
  • We utilize technology like IoT, drones, and satellite imaging for planning and monitoring, and Blockchain transparency for accountability.

Our Impact

Palizi Farmland is committed to empowering farmers, regenerating land, and growing wealth. Our 3-year outcomes demonstrate significant impact:

  • 5,000+ acres regenerated.
  • 15,000+ trained individuals.
  • 1M+ trees planted.
  • 30%+ income increases for participants.

Get Involved: Palizi Farmland is actively seeking funding, grants, climate and agri-tech mentors, carbon certification partners, tech development support, and market access/distribution partnerships.
